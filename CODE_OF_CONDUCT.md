# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free community for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent while giving clear technical feedback.
- Focus on ideas, code, and outcomes, not personal attacks.
- Respect boundaries and privacy.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, hate speech, discrimination, or intimidation.
- Personal attacks, trolling, or sustained disruption.
- Publishing private information without explicit consent.

## Reporting

Report issues to the maintainers via the security/support contacts documented in `SECURITY.md` and `SUPPORT.md`.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove or edit comments, reject contributions, or temporarily/permanently restrict participation for behavior that violates this code.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to project spaces, issue/PR discussions, and related community interactions.
